About & Help

About SmartZone AI

SmartZone AI is an advanced safety monitoring system that uses artificial intelligence to detect potential hazards and security threats in real-time. Our system analyzes video and audio feeds to identify incidents such as fires, accidents, falls, crowd formations, and distress signals.

With cutting-edge computer vision and audio analysis algorithms, SmartZone AI helps organizations enhance safety, reduce response times, and prevent accidents before they escalate.

Getting Started

To begin using SmartZone AI:

  1. Configure your camera feeds in the Settings section
  2. Set up notification preferences for your team
  3. Adjust detection thresholds according to your environment
  4. Monitor real-time feeds in the Live Monitoring section
  5. Review alerts and analytics to improve safety protocols

For optimal performance, ensure cameras have clear views of monitored areas and adequate lighting conditions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the detection system?

Our AI models achieve over 95% accuracy in controlled environments. Accuracy may vary based on camera quality and environmental factors.

Can I use multiple cameras?

Yes, SmartZone AI supports unlimited cameras. Add additional feeds in the Camera Configuration settings.

What are the system requirements?

SmartZone AI runs in modern web browsers. For best performance, we recommend Chrome or Firefox with hardware acceleration enabled.
